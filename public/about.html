Our Mission

At West Message, our mission is to empower businesses with innovative communication solutions. We specialize in driving repeat sales and building customer relationships through the combined power of scalable text messaging, generative AI, and automated workflows.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Scalable Text Messaging: We specialize in simplifying communication. With just a few clicks, you can effortlessly reach all of your past clients to drive repeat sales, inform them of time-sensitive events, and even provide a convenient text-to-order system.
  • Customized Solutions: We understand that every business is unique. That's why we offer tailor-made solutions, including AI-powered chatbots that field questions, book appointments, and automate customer interactions.
  • Innovative Voice Integration: Our integration with voice assistants like Siri takes convenience to the next level. Imagine customers placing orders with a simple "Hey, Siri..." command. It's all possible with West Message.
