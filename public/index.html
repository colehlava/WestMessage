Connect with your audience through simple, scalable texting.

How does it work?


  1. We give you a phone number.

  2. Anyone can opt-in to your messaging list by texting that number.

  3. You log in, type a message, and send to your audience.

Targeted Communication

  • Client grouping features let you send targeted messages to specific audiences.

  • Schedule messages to send at a specific time and day.

Seamless Integration

  • Integrate directly with your Shopify store or CRM.

  • Effortlessly send a text message to your audience.
